Who We Are

We are a team of experienced online learning consultants dedicated to creating excellent learning experiences.

We Know Our Craft

We understand the unique challenges and opportunities that educational institutions face today as they establish themselves in the online learning space.

Our consultants bring years of experience in online learning, ensuring that every project we undertake is grounded in best practices and tailored to meet the specific needs of our clients. We work collaboratively to develop learning solutions that are not only effective but also engaging and accessible to all learners.

From Plan to Perfection

Our expertise extends beyond online course development to include comprehensive project management. We recognize that delivering high-quality educational solutions requires meticulous planning, coordination, and execution.

Our project managers (PMs) are adept at overseeing complex projects, ensuring that they are completed on time, within scope and budget, and to the highest standards of quality.

We’re Here to Help

At Manara, we work as an extension of your team, completely aligned with your processes and objectives. Think of us as an extra set of hands—ready, reliable, and always on the same page.
